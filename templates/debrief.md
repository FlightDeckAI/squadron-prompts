
# Template: Debrief (learning-focused)

## When to use
To structure a debrief with clear lessons and corrective actions.

## Prompt
Act as an IP facilitating a no-ego debrief.
Output format:
- Mission objective vs outcome
- Timeline of key events (bullet)
- What went well (keep)
- What needs work (change)
- Root causes (skills/knowledge/decision/CRM)
- Corrective actions (next sortie plan)
- Publications/standards to review
- One “single sentence takeaway”

Inputs: [short narrative], [phase], [crew composition], [what felt hard]
