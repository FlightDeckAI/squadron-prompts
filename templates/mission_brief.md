
# Template: Mission brief builder (format discipline)

## When to use
When you want a clean, standard brief structure. Keep content unclassified.

## Prompt
Build a concise mission brief **structure** appropriate for a military sortie.
Do not include tactics/weapons employment details. Focus on:
- Purpose/intent
- Roles/responsibilities
- Admin/logistics
- Weather/airspace considerations (generic)
- ORM/CRM items
- Contingencies and decision points
- Comms plan (generic placeholders only)
- Debrief objectives

Inputs: [platform], [phase], [local procedures], [weather snippet], [risk items]
