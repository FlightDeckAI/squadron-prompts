
# Mission planning, ORM, and ADM prompts (unclassified)

## Prompts (1–15)
1. Build an ORM-style risk assessment for this sortie: [phase], [crew], [weather summary], [schedule], [threats = generic]. Identify top 5 mitigations.
2. Convert this situation into a TEM brief: [situation]. Output threats/errors/UAS and countermeasures.
3. Create a “go / modify / no‑go” decision tree for [training objective] with conservative decision points.
4. Write three professional ways to push back on schedule pressure while maintaining mission focus.
5. I’m experiencing task saturation in [phase]. Provide a reset plan using aviate-navigate-communicate and CRM callouts.
6. Draft a crew contract for this sortie: roles, expectations, callouts, and “knock it off” criteria.
7. Identify the highest-risk transitions in this profile (e.g., takeoff/low level/approach) and propose briefing items to protect them.
8. Create a short “plan B” section for: divert, weather deterioration, comm failure, and return-to-base priorities (generic).
9. Provide a checklist for preventing “normalization of deviance” in routine sorties.
10. Turn these notes into a one-page brief card: [notes].
11. Create a personal minimums matrix for [day/night], [IMC/VMC], [single/dual], [experience level].
12. Give me a habit loop to improve mission planning discipline in 10 minutes/day.
13. Produce a conservative passenger/crew brief for a high-workload sortie (generic).
14. Help me identify cognitive biases affecting my decision here: [scenario]. Provide counter-bias techniques.
15. Build a “before stepping” checklist for sortie day: sleep, hydration, admin, pubs, data, equipment (generic).

## OPSEC note
Keep scenarios unclassified; do not include codes/frequencies/tactics.
